Text-Trade

Text-Trade is an iOS based mobile application that allows college students to buy and sell textbooks directly to each other. It helps students by getting them the textbooks they need for the fairest price possible. This will help not only the buyers of used textbooks, but also those that are looking to sell the ones they no longer need.
